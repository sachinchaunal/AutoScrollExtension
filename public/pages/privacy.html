At AutoScroll Extension, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in, we collect your email address, name, and profile picture from your Google account
  • Payment Information: UPI ID and payment transaction details (processed securely through Razorpay)
  • Contact Information: Name and email when you contact our support team

Usage Information

  • Extension Usage: When you activate/deactivate AutoScroll functionality
  • Subscription Data: Trial status, subscription status, and payment history
  • Technical Data: Browser version, extension version, and error logs (anonymized)

Device Information

  • Browser Data: Chrome extension ID and version
  • System Information: Operating system type (for installer compatibility)

2. How We Use Your Information

Primary Purposes:
  • Provide and maintain the AutoScroll Extension service
  • Process subscription payments and manage billing
  • Authenticate users and prevent unauthorized access
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send important updates about your account or service changes
  • Improve extension functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure Hosting: Data is stored on secure MongoDB Atlas servers
  • Payment Security: All payment processing is handled by Razorpay (PCI DSS compliant)
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Payment History: Retained for 7 years for legal and tax purposes
  • Support Communications: Retained for 2 years
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymized data retained indefinitely for service improvement

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT:
  • Sell or rent your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data for marketing purposes
  • Track your browsing activity outside of YouTube Shorts
  • Access or store your YouTube viewing history

Limited Data Sharing

  • Payment Processing: UPI payment data shared with Razorpay for transaction processing
  • Authentication: Google OAuth for secure sign-in (no data shared with Google beyond authentication)
  • Legal Requirements: Data may be disclosed if required by law or legal process

5. Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your account information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format

Subscription Management

  • Cancel Anytime: Cancel your subscription without penalty
  • Payment Control: Manage your UPI autopay mandate
  • Data After Cancellation: Account data deleted 30 days after cancellation

6. Cookies and Local Storage

  • Local Storage: Extension stores authentication tokens locally in your browser
  • Session Data: Temporary session data for website functionality
  • No Tracking Cookies: We do not use tracking or advertising cookies

7. Third-Party Services

Integrated Services

  • Google OAuth: For secure authentication - Google Privacy Policy
  • Razorpay: For payment processing - Razorpay Privacy Policy
  • MongoDB Atlas: For secure data storage - MongoDB Privacy Policy

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this privacy policy.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Compliance

  • GDPR Compliance: For EU users, we comply with General Data Protection Regulation
  • CCPA Compliance: For California users, we comply with California Consumer Privacy Act
  • Indian Data Protection: We comply with applicable Indian data protection laws
